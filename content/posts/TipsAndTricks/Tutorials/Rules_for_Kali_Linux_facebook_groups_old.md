## 1. Focus on Learning and Skill Development:
The primary focus of this group is to provide a platform for discussions, sharing, and collaboration related to ethical hacking, cybersecurity and Kali Linux. Members are encouraged to ask questions and share their knowledge and experiences to improve their skills and understanding of these topics.

All posts related to these topics are welcome and encouraged. But asking for assistance with basic errors in Kali Linux may not be approved. So, please ask in the Community Chat.

## 2. Ethical Behavior:
It is strictly prohibited to engage in any kind of scam or fraudulent activity that aims to gain unauthorized access to accounts or personal information. Members are expected to behave ethically and respect others' privacy and security. Always ask for permission before attempting any activity that involves someone else's account or personal information. Do not spread Copyright material. We will not discuss or help with any inquiries about "hacking" social accounts, Email accts (incl "recovery")

## 3. No Scams or Paid Promotions:
The promotion of scams or paid services is strictly prohibited in this Kali Linux group. Posts containing Short URL, promotional content for commercial services or products will be removed and repeat offenders may be banned from the group. Before posting, please consult with a Moderator if you are unsure if your content is appropriate. This group aims to provide valuable information and resources to its members, and promoting scams or paid services goes against its purpose.

## 4. Guidelines for creating error post:
When creating an error post, please include a clear and concise description of the error, along with a screenshot, the name of the tool, and, if it's not available in the Kali repository, provide the source link. This will help the community better understand the problem. Don't forget to include the steps that led to this error and any troubleshooting attempts you made. These pieces of information will help the community to understand the problem in-depth and provide relevant solutions.

## 5. Respectful Interaction:
Please treat all members of this group with respect and kindness. Harassment, hate speech, bullying, sexually motivated comments, or discriminatory remarks of any kind are strictly prohibited. Do not harass or insult other members. Violating this rule will result in an instant ban.

## 6. Communication Language:
To ensure that all members can participate in discussions and understand each other, this group is based on the English language. All posts should be written in English. Posts in any other language may not be approved.

## 7. Privacy:
Respect the privacy of all group members. Do not share any personal information, including phone numbers, email addresses, or any other sensitive details. Solicitation of private messages or friend requests without the explicit permission of the person you wish to contact is strictly prohibited. Violation of this rule may result in removal from the group.

## 8. Admin and Moderator Respect:
Admins and Moderators play a crucial role in enhancing the community whenever they find free time. Their dedication contributes significantly to creating a better environment. Please show care and respect towards them for their valuable contributions.
Remember, displaying toxic behavior or disrespecting them may lead you to suspend and even permanent ban.
If you believe they are misusing their power, you can report the creator of this community, Gajendra Mahato, at Facebook.com/GajendraMahato.sh
